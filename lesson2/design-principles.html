Fitt's Law

YouTube

youtube.com

Fitt's Law refers to the relationship between the size of a target, its distance from the user, and the time required to move to the target accurately. The more noticeable a button is and the closer together all the functions the user needs to access are, the better the experience and functionality of the webpage. I believe that YouTube exemplifies Fitt's law becuase everything a user needs to access while watching a video is there on the same screen with no scrolling required. The play/pause button, settings, autoplay, and timeline scrubber are large, noticeable, and easily clickable, allowing users to interact with them easily. These elements are strategically positioned at the bottom of the player, reducing the required cursor/finger movement.

Visual Hierarchy

Dropbox

dropbox.com

Visual hierarchy is one of the most important design principle in my opinion becuase it helps guide the user's attention and priorities on a webpage. Start with the most important things being the most visable so that the user moves their eyes and attention according to the size of the headlines or the buttons which should be arranged from most important to least important. I believe that Dropbox exemplifies this principle on their webpage becuase the user's attention starts with the largest headline which informs them of the 700 million user community that backs the company and trusts them. The next thing they notice is the button to sign up which informs them that the service is free, followed by the smaller text inbetween the button and headline that describes other benefits and perks of Dropbox.

Contrast

Apple

apple.com

Contrast is another key principle of web design. It focuses on creating visual differences between elements to make them stand out more to the user. Apple utilizes this principle well by using a black background with white lettering, then switching to a white background with black lettering. Users also can't help but notice the sharp purple, yellow, red, and blue colors of the iPhones in the pictures. It creates a great modern style and draws attention to everything on the page through color.
