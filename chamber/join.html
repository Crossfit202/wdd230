Membership Benefits

NP Membership (Non-Profit Organization) ▾

  • Free membership - No Fees!
  • Access to networking events and business forums.
  • Inclusion in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other non-profit organizations.
  • Listing of events and fundraisers in the Chamber's newsletter.
  • Access to educational resources and training materials.
Close

Bronze Membership ▾

  • Basic membership package - $100
  • All benefits of NP Membership.
  • Opportunity to attend Chamber networking events and workshops.
  • Listing in the Chamber's online directory with an enhanced profile.
  • Access to member-exclusive business resources and tools.
  • Discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored events and programs.
  • Ability to participate in Chamber committees and task forces.
Close

Silver Membership ▾

  • Enhanced membership package - $500
  • All benefits of Bronze Membership.
  • Priority listing in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Increased visibility through featured spotlights on the Chamber's website.
  • Exclusive access to industry-specific workshops and seminars.
  • Increased promotion through the Chamber's social media channels.
  • Access to market research and economic reports.
  • Sponsorship opportunities for Chamber events.
Close

Gold Membership ▾

  • Premium membership package - $1000
  • All benefits of Silver Membership.
  • Premium placement in the Chamber's online directory.
  • Prominent recognition on the Chamber's website homepage.
  • Priority access to Chamber leadership and influential members.
  • VIP invitations to exclusive events and receptions.
  • Access to executive-level networking opportunities.
  • Customized marketing and promotional opportunities.
  • Recognition as a major supporter of the Chamber's initiatives.
Close
