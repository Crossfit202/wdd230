Econimic Impact

From 2019 to 2020, employment in Grove City, OH grew at a rate of 4.05%, from 21.4k employees to 22.3k employees. The most common job groups, by number of people living in Grove City, OH, are Office & Administrative Support Occupations (3,691 people), Management Occupations (2,636 people), and Sales & Related Occupations (2,086 people).
