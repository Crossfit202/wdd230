Business Mixer

Join us for an exclusive Business Networking Mixer, where local entrepreneurs, professionals, and business owners come together to build connections and explore new opportunities. This event provides a platform for networking, sharing ideas, and fostering collaborations within our vibrant business community. Whether you're a seasoned business owner or just starting out, this event offers a valuable opportunity to expand your network and meet like-minded individuals in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. Highlights of the event: Engage in meaningful conversations with influential figures in the local business community. Discover potential partnerships, collaborations, and business prospects. Enjoy delicious appetizers and refreshments while networking. Participate in interactive activities designed to facilitate connections. Exchange business cards and expand your contact list. Gain insights from guest speakers who will share their expertise on relevant business topics. Raffle prizes and giveaways for attendees.

  • Date: May 26th, 2023
  • Location: Gantz Park
  • Registration: Register Online Today! | Register Now

Don't miss out on this fantastic opportunity to connect, collaborate, and grow your business network. Join us at the Business Networking Mixer and unlock new possibilities for success in your entrepreneurial journey.
