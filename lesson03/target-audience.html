Target Audience

Our target audience includes local business owners, managers, entrepreneurs, startups, and professionals who are looking for resources/support to grow and succeed. We also aim to engage with community members who are interested in supporting local businesses and fostering economic development in the area. Finally, our website serves as a valuable resource for visitors to the region who are looking to connect with local businesses and organizations so they can learn more about the unique features and benefits of our community.

Persona 1

"Alex the Entrepreneur"

  • Name: Alex Johnson
  • Job title / major responsibilities: Founder and CEO of a software startup
  • Age: 29
  • Marriage Status: Single
  • Gender: Male
  • Goals and Tasks: Alex is focused on growing his startup and securing funding. He is always on the lookout for new business opportunities and ways to improve his product. He spends his time networking with other entrepreneurs, attending startup events, and working on his product development.
  • Enviroment: Alex spends most of his workday in front of a computer, managin his business using software tools. He is very comfortable using technology and relies on it to stay connected and informed.
  • Quote: "I'm excited to be part of the local business community and to connect with other entrepreneurs in the area."

Persona 2

"Community-Minded Resident"

  • Name: Maria Rodriguez
  • Job title / major responsibilities: Homemaker
  • Age: 38
  • Marriage Status: Married with 2 children
  • Gender: Female
  • Goals and Tasks: Maria is passionate about supporting her local community and wants to stay informed about events, initiatives, and businesses in the area. She spends her time volunteering at local charities, attending community events, and connecting with other residents.
  • Enviroment: Maria uses the internet and social media to stay connected with friends, family, and local organizations. She is comfortable using a computer but does not consider herself a tech expert.
  • Quote: "I'm excited to see what the Chamber of Commerce is doing to support our community. I want to stay informed and be part of the conversation."

Scenarios

  1. A small business owner in the area is looking for local suppliers or service providers for their business. They use the website's business directory to find potential vendors, check their reviews, and contact them for further information.
  2. A new resident in the area is looking to get involved in the community and meet new people. They use the website's community events calendar to find upcoming events such as festivals, farmer's markets, and concerts.
  3. An aspiring entrepreneur is interested in starting a business in the area but is unsure of where to begin. They use the website to find resources such as business planning guides, funding sources, and mentorship programs.
  4. A family is considering relocating to the area for a new job opportunity. They use the site to find information on housing, schools, and local amenities such as parks and libraries.
  5. A family is planning a vacation to the area and wants to learn more about local attractions and activities. They use the website to find information on popular tourist destinations, accommodations, restaurants, and events. The website also provides a downloadable visitor's guide and interactive map to help them plan their trip.
