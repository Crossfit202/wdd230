The South Coast Region

This region of California spans from Ventura County to the Mexican Border encompassing Los Angeles, Orange, and San Diego counties. Known for sandy beaches, excellent waves for surfing, beautiful climate, and a diverse population.

Southern California is home to approximately 24 million people, making it the location of the two largest cities on the West Coast: Los Angeles and San Diego.
